Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy my own domain name?

Yes, you will register your domain name with a provider like Google Domains or GoDaddy. After you register it, we will provide you with the necessary DNS settings to point your domain to your new website. This gives you full ownership and control of your domain while we handle all the technical hosting details.

What does the subscription cover?

Our subscription includes everything you need to have a professional online presence. This covers the initial design and development of your website, secure hosting, ongoing maintenance, and a set number of content updates per month to keep your site fresh.

How do I request content updates?

Requesting updates is simple. Just send us an email with the changes you need, whether it's updating your business hours, adding a new item to your menu, or changing a photo. We'll make the changes for you and let you know as soon as they're live.

What if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Simply contact us and we'll begin the cancellation process. Once your subscription is cancelled, your website will be taken offline.

Since your domain is separate, you'll still own it and can use it for a new website in the future.

Can I switch my subscription tier?

Of course! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time to better suit your business needs. Just contact our support team and we will guide you through the process and adjust your service accordingly.

In order to prevent chaos, we are going to limit the switching once bi-monthly.
